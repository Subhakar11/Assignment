About Us

Apple Inc. is a global technology company renowned for its innovation in consumer electronics, software, and services. Founded in 1976, it revolutionized personal computing with the Macintosh and later transformed mobile communications with the iPhone. Apple continues to lead with products like iPads, Macs, and services like iCloud and Apple Music.

Our Services

Apple provides comprehensive customer service through Apple Support, Genius Bar appointments at Apple Stores, and online resources. Services include technical support, repairs, and troubleshooting for all Apple products. AppleCare+ offers extended warranty and support plans, ensuring reliable assistance and peace of mind for users worldwide.

Contact Us

To contact Apple for support or inquiries, visit their official website and navigate to the "Contact Apple Support" section. You can also reach out via phone at 1-800-MY-APPLE (1-800-692-7753) for assistance with products, orders, or technical issues. Additionally, Apple offers support through their Apple Support app and social media channels like Twitter (@AppleSupport).
